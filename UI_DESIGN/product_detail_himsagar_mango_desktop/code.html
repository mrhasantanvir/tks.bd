The Rajshahi Harvest Cycle

Our Himsagar mangoes follow a strictly traditional ripening schedule. Located in the fertile belt of Chapainawabganj and Rajshahi, these orchards benefit from unique soil composition that creates the signature honey-sweet flavor.

The cycle begins with "Mukul" in February, followed by careful nurturing through the nor'westers of April. We wait until the mangoes reach "Poka" (maturity) on the tree before hand-picking them during the first week of June. This ensures you get fruit that hasn't been chemically induced to ripen early.
